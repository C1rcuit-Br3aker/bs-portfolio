PerfectR Showcase

Nothing better than this

The American Adventurer's Guild was founded to re-introduce some adventure back into our lives. We believe that Adventure, thrills, and freedom from boredom are not extras in life, but necissary for happiness. For that reason we design adventures in the form of treasure hunts for our customers..... hunts for real treasure like bullion and gems, as well as artifacts and artwork. During your experience you will solve clues, research history, and with every step come closer to finding your treasure! And once you've found it, it's yours to keep, and you will have something forever that you can tell your friends and family about, or just cash in and take the money!

This was my final project that was built using all the tools I learned at The Iron Yard. I worked on a team, collaborating to build the site from conception to the final product. We mapped and tracked our progress using Trello, setting daily and weekly goals over the course of 3 weeks. The main tools we used were HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Ember. We also used Node, SLC Loopback to build an API, Ember addons like Ember Paper and Liquid Fire. We deployed the site using Surge.
